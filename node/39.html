The Foundation for Creating Great Digital Experiences

Drupal is a powerful open-source content management system (CMS) that provides a strong foundation for your web application or online business. It is also the second most commonly used CMS on the Internet after Wordpress. Drupal has a large network of developers around the world who are constantly making updates and improvements to the platform.

Who Uses Drupal

Coplex is a Drupal Association member and we have tons of experience creating robust web applications as well as informational, e-commerce and enterprise sites. The platform significantly cuts time in development over custom platforms and provides a powerful backend management system to control site users, permissions and content. Drupal sites also come out of the box with strong SEO (Search Engine Optimization) potential.

Each Drupal project at Coplex is designed and built from scratch based on our clients’ needs. There are very few limitations to what can be done with Drupal because of the approach we take. Our talented team works together seamlessly to architect, design and develop the perfect custom solution.

The process starts with a discovery, during which our team develops a roadmap for the project based on your customers, business goals, timeframes and budget. After the discovery stage is complete, our architects and designs work together to bring your brand to life with a combination of art and science.

Finally, our talented Drupal developers implement the designs into the CMS using the best tools to fit your project. Depending on your project’s technical requirements, our team may use time-saving modules from the Drupal community or develop modules from scratch to meet your exact requirements.

After your site is up and running, we work with your team to show you how to easily manage your site and content. Our Drupal maintenance team can also help with future enhancements and bug fixes as your site grows. And our online marketing team can use SEO and online advertising services to drive targeted and relevant traffic to your new site.
