Web & Mobile Maintenance

   Back

It’s all about having the latest and greatest. We monitor your activity and user feedback to make strategic decisions and updates. This keeps you up to date, ahead of your competition and in front of your customers. 

Our technology is constantly evolving

Most websites have a variety of modules, plug-ins and other pieces working on the back-end to enhance its functionality. These additional pieces can become dated over time as developers and designers continue to innovate and push the web forward. In order to ensure that your website continues to provide value and a positive user experience, you need to regularly service and maintain it.

We improve integrations based on new technology, manage application updates and pay attention to how users navigate your site. It’s important to keep a close eye on these components to ensure that your users have the best possible experience on your website.

CMS platforms are built to make your life easier

Many content management systems such as Drupal, Magento and WordPress also have regular updates in order to provide improved capabilities and stability for their users. This is a really great feature of the platforms we work with, and one of the reasons we’ve taken to creating websites using these tools. Web and mobile applications also require frequent updates to address bug fixes and new features.

Once your site is live and starts to attract more visitors, we provide functionality enhancements, bug fixes and whatever else it may need. A large part of the Agile philosophy is working in iterations, which means constantly improving your site with each update.

Regularly scheduled maintenance and updates should be made to all web products. We can help you monitor and make informed decisions and updates based on this information. This shows that you’re listening and that you care about your users, just like we do with you.
