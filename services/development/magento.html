Reinventing the Social Shopping Experience

Magento is a flexible and easy-to-use eCommerce platform used by over 200,000 merchants around the world. Building on this platform is fast because it’s high performing, scalable and created to be the optimal eCommerce solution for online stores.

Who Uses Magento

Coplex has a wide range of experience in creating enterprise-level Magento sites. We know that online stores can have high-volume and grow quickly which is easily supported by the Magento framework.

Coplex works with you to develop your enterprise or community eCommerce site so you can rest assured you’ll receive only the highest quality of service. Our talented team works together seamlessly to architect, design and develop a custom solution to fit your needs.

1 out of 4 online businesses choose Magento

300+ solution & industry partners

$25+ billion in transactions annually

The process starts with an initial discovery where we work with your team to understand your brand, products and customers. From there, we develop the ideal flows, product configurations, site map and mobile integration, with checkpoints for you every step of the way. After the discovery and architecture stages are complete, our talented designers bring your brand to life using a combination of art and science.

Our skilled development team brings all the work of the architects and designers a reality. Depending on the type of functionality, our developers will either create custom modules or use templates to maximize the efficiency of the process.

Once your Magento site is up-and-running, our team will work with you to get you trained and feeling comfortable with your new online store. And afterwards, we have a full array of online marketing services to kick-start your new website and drive customers to your online store.
