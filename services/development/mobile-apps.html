Mobile Applications

   Back

People spend over 2 hours using mobile apps every day

With the average population spending over two hours using mobile apps every day, it’s hard to ignore their importance. Mobile applications range from optimized mobile versions of eCommerce storefronts, mobile front-ends for web applications, or a set of tools for customers to use on their tablet devices. Coplex has the experience and capability to create these mobile apps for iOS devices such as iPhones and iPads, as well as Android-powered handheld and tablet devices.

With development, you have the choice between individual native applications for each operating system or cross-platform development software like Xamarin to save time in development. Many of the mobile applications we build rely on a web services layer or an API to connect to a web application backend.

Mobile applications require a heavy emphasis on user-experience and design

Mobile design encompasses a very different set of design requirements with touch-friendly displays, multiple screen orientations, animated transitions, and special user-interface elements. Beyond that, each operating system also has unique design best practices that need to be followed.

When mobile application projects start at Coplex, they begin in a discovery phase. During this part of the project, we put together a project brief based on your customers, business goals, timeframes and budget.

We often work with businesses in the startup phase to define the MVP (Minimal Viable Product) following the Lean Startup principles. The goal of an MVP is to minimize the time and cost to get your application live. The MVP is often a simplified version of your end product used to validate the concept and iterate the idea based on real user feedback.

We bring your brand to life with a combination of art and science

After the discovery stage is complete, our strategists and designers work together to bring your brand to life with a combination of art and science. Once the designs are complete, the design and development teams work together to efficiently transition your project development.

From there, our team of mobile app developers in Los Angeles and Phoenix work with you to build out the designs and flows into a functional application. We test the app and provide you the ability to download, install and test the app on your device. Upon approval we will work with you to submit the app to the App Store and Google Play Store.
