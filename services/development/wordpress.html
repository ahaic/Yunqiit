The Internet's Most Widely Used Content Management System

WordPress is a free and open-source content management system (CMS) and the most widely used system on the Internet. With an easy-to-manage administrative backend, a great set of out-of-the-box features and a variety of widgets, WordPress has grown to be the platform of choice for a wide variety of sites including blogs and professional websites.

Who Uses WordPress

At Coplex, we specialize in designing and developing custom WordPress blogs, websites and lightweight web applications. Our team can build WordPress sites for small businesses, startups, large enterprises, academic institutions and non-profit organizations.

You should see what we can do! Our experienced WordPress team is trained on the platform’s best practices and they follow new developments regularly, all to ensure that your site is easy to manage, secure, and scalable. Our team handles full site builds as well as maintenance on existing WordPress sites.

Think a WordPress site is right for you? The process for a new site build on WordPress starts with an initial discovery where we work with your team to understand your brand, products and customers. From there, we develop the ideal flows, product configurations, site map and mobile integration, with checkpoints for you every step of the way. After the discovery and architecture stages are complete, our talented designers bring your brand to life using a combination of art and science.

Finally, our skilled development team makes all the hard work of the architects and designers into a reality. Depending on the agreed upon technical requirements, we select the appropriate extensions and third-party tools for your site. In many cases, we will create custom extensions to implement more complex site functionality and integrations.

Once your WordPress site is up-and-running, we will work with your team to train you so you’re comfortable navigating your new site. You’ll have complete control of your own place on the web, and the fun doesn’t end there, though. We have an array of online marketing solutions to kick-start your new website and bring customers to your perfect presence online.
