Online Marketing Services

   Back

Effective online marketing is the result of purposeful integration of multiple strategies to increase sales, visibility and traffic for your business online. At Coplex, we specialize in Online Advertising, Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Social Media Marketing. We're the online marketing company that can help drive real ROI for your business.

Drive targeted and relevant traffic to your website

Online Advertising allows you to specifically target customers based on criteria like demographics, location, device type, browsing behavior, and even social media engagement. Coplex provides online marketing services such as pay-per-click advertising, display advertising, retargeting, social advertising, product listing ads and nontraditional advertising. With the help of online advertising you can quickly drive targeted and relevant traffic to your website or online storefront to drive new business.

For new projects, we start with an online marketing discovery to better understand your business goals, customers, and revenue models. This allows us to build a truly custom online marketing campaign for you. From there, we decide the best ongoing plan that fits your budget and long-term goals.

Improve search ranking 

Search Engine Optimization improves your ranking in search as well as your overall online presence, so you can acquire new customers simply by being your unique self. Coplex provides a blended on-site and off-site SEO offering that meets the varied needs of your business. Our team works with you to build up your online authority, manage your link profile, and improve your website itself so that you can rank for terms that lead you to more sales and exposure.

Once we engage in a monthly management campaign, we work seamlessly with your team to keep you informed throughout the process. We deliver monthly reports and schedule recurring monthly review calls where we discuss the campaign progress. Based on the results, we will work together with you to address any changes that need to be made to the campaign.

Increase brand awareness

Social Media Marketing is based on relationship building, content sharing and reputation management. It allows you to develop a community and increase more brand awareness among your current and potential customers. Social media platforms are constantly evolving, making it imperative to be aware of the latest platforms and how they can grow your company.
